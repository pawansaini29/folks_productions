More About Us

Capturing moments, crafting stories!!

Welcome to Folks Productions, where we specialize in the art of capturing moments through the lenses of our creative visionaries. As a dedicated team of six photographers and videographers, all having academic qualifications from the esteemed Indian Institute of Photography, we bring a blend of technical expertise and artistic flair to every project.

At Folks Productions, we excel in fashion shoots, product and food photography, and immersive event coverage. Our approach is rooted in understanding your story—whether it's a brand's journey, a culinary masterpiece, or a special event—and translating it into compelling visual narratives.

Having collaborated with industry leaders like Granmade, Up To Trends, Brandent, and others, we pride ourselves on delivering not just outstanding work, but also a seamless client experience. Our portfolio reflects our commitment to quality, punctuality, and the unique essence of each project.

Driven by a passion for creativity and a deep appreciation for our craft, we go beyond the brief to capture the true essence of your vision. Whether it's through a virtual consultation or a face-to-face meeting, we ensure that every detail is meticulously crafted to exceed your expectations. Join us at Folks Productions, where we turn moments into memories and visions into reality. We look forward to collaborating with you and building lasting connections through our shared passion for storytelling through visuals.
